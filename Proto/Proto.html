ESP MUSE PROTO

The ESP Muse Proto is an audio development board with a mono speaker, RGB LED, MEMS microphone, and 18650 battery support. Designed for DIY projects and hackable applications using the ESP32, it's compatible with Arduino IDE.

Key Specifications:

  • 3W mono output
  • On-board speaker
  • Micro-USB charging/programming port
  • 1 MEMS microphone
  • Programmable RGB LED (WS2812)
  • 18650 battery slot (battery not included)
  • Dimensions: 10 x 5 x 1 cm

ESP MUSE Mn Cast

The Mn Cast is designed to upgrade your old HiFi system with modern features like Bluetooth, AirPlay, local streaming, and Home Assistant support. It comes with a high-quality 32-bit 384kHz DAC and is powered by the ESP32 Wrover.

Key Specifications:

  • 32-bit 384kHz DAC
  • Analog jack output (line level)
  • Optical digital output (mini-TOSLINK)
  • Bluetooth 4.2 LE
  • 1.3-inch monochrome OLED display
  • USB-C, SD card, and infrared receiver
  • Programmable RGB LED
  • Rotary encoder with push button

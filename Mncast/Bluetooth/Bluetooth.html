This is the tutorial for the ESP Mn cast to load the Basic bluetooth speaker sketch. This is the default Sketch that is loaded on the speaker when you received it.

Features of this Squetch:

  • Bluetooth AD2P audio connectivity
  • Display
  • MP3 read on the SD card
  • Battery neopixel charge indicator
  • Mute button, volume UP, Down

ESP MUSE LUXE

The ESP Muse Luxe is a hackable 10W Bluetooth speaker based on the ESP32. It supports AirPlay, Bluetooth, Logitech Media Server, and SD card MP3 playback. Easily programmable using the Arduino IDE, it comes with tutorials and example applications.

Key Specifications:

  • 2x 5W stereo output
  • USB-C charging
  • 1 MEMS microphone
  • Programmable RGB LED
  • 18650 rechargeable battery (6h playing time)
  • Dimensions: 23 x 8 x 6 cm

MuseRadio - Radio stable

Stable, radio-only firmware for Muse Radio.

Build 3a75544 from the radio-only-stable branch.

  • Internet radio with local presets
  • Local Wi-Fi and station management portal
  • Up to 99 presets and manual station URL entry
  • ES8388 codec volume, mute and headphone/speaker control

Full installation: this test image replaces saved Wi-Fi and station data.

After installation, connect to MuseRadio-XXXX with password museradio, then open http://192.168.4.1.
